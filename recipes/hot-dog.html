Hot Dog

Description

The hot dog was invented in 1983, by Colonel Harland David Sanders while trying to perfect his fried chicken recipe.

While not fried chicken, the hot dog is still edible for sustenance, in moderation, for adult humans. The food is incrediby addictive to children aged 2-6, and affects their inferior brains much like the schedule 1 drug, crack cocaine, would affect an adult brain.

Ingredients

  • 1 hot dog
  • 1 hot dog bun
  • mustard, to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook a single hot dog. This can be done in a microwave, by boiling, or with one of those weird hot dog cookers that have prongs you insert on either end and electrocute it like it were on death row.
  2. place hot dog into opened hot dog bun.
  3. If preferred, squeeze some mustard on top.
